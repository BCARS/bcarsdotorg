A meeting of the Bedford County Amateur Radio Society was called to order at **1930** hours at **Bedford American Legion** by **President Zachary Pepple, KC3KHK** and a quorum was declared.

In attendance were (**9**): <!--more--> John KB3DFZ, Lloyd K3QNT, Steve KE3ZT, Bernie W3DRW, Zach KC3KHK, Ellis KC3WMI, Kip K3IP, Jay K3SCM, and Gary KC3HKZ

**Meeting Minutes** were presented by John KB3DFZ. Motion to approve was made by **K3QNT** and seconded by **W3DRW**.

**Treasurer's report was given by K3QNT.**  Motion to approve was made by **KE3ZT**, seconded by **W3DRW**.

## Old Business

**Halloween Parade** K3IP will drive the Van. Parade is October 27th, Monday at 7:30PM. Rain date is October 30th. Time to report is at 6:30PM. Please let Kip know if you will help out.

**Repeater Update** No work has been done since the September workshop.

## New Business

**National Weather Service** of State College is having a workshop on Monday, October 20th at 10AM. All hams are invited to attend.

**Field Day Discussed** K3QNT reports that Winter Field Day location will be the Shawnee lodge again. For Summer Field Day, reservations have been made at the Shawnee East Pavilion 10. 

**WPA Set Exercise** KB3DFZ reports no guidance has been sent regarding the fall set exercise. BCARS will plan to operate a check-in net at 10AM, Saturday, October 4th on the 145.490 repeater.


With no further business, the meeting was adjourned at **2010** hours.

Respectfully Submitted,  


John Hogenmiller, KB3DFZ  
BCARS Secretary
